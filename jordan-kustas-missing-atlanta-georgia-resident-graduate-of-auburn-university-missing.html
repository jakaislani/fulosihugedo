    Jordan Kustas Missing Person – Our profound sorrow and heavy hearts are filled with regret to inform you of the mysterious whereabouts of our cherished sibling, Jordan Kustas. Jordan, who graduated from Auburn University at the tender age of 23, had since established himself and begun to shape his life in the Buckhead neighborhood of Atlanta. Jordan’s most recent correspondence to us was dated November 15, and he has not been sighted or heard from since then.

    His lack of presence has caused a profound vacuum in our lives, as he has been unable to celebrate important occasions such as his birthday, Thanksgiving, and Christmas. Due to the uncertainty surrounding his health, the past few weeks have been extraordinarily difficult for our family. Notwithstanding the submission of a missing person report, the constraints imposed by his age have posed challenges in commencing thorough search endeavors. Friends and members of the broader community are urged to disseminate this information, specifically in the Atlanta region, as a collective endeavor to reunite us with Jordan.

    Any lead, regardless of its magnitude, may prove indispensable in aiding our search for him and guaranteeing his safety. Brooks, Jordan’s devoted golden retriever, is frequently spotted accompanying him, and he operates a 2013 silver Hyundai Tucson bearing North Carolina license plates. We are requesting any individuals who have recently encountered him or been in contact with him to share any information that could provide insight into his current location.

    Please feel at liberty to reach out to Peter Harrison or Michelle Scarborough Harrison for any pertinent information. Your support may serve as the ray of optimism that we so urgently require during this arduous period. Our sole desire is to regain Jordan’s companionship and assure him of their continued affection. We draw strength from the affection and support of our family, friends, and community during this difficult time. Jordan, your presence is greatly missed, and we maintain optimism regarding your imminent return.
