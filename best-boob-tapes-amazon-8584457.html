    Bras are interesting. We’ve finally entered a time where brands are prioritizing comfort and support over looks, but the undergarments can still be itchy, tight, and restrictive — and don’t even get me started on those slipping straps. That’s why I’m obsessed with boob tape; it’s exactly what it sounds like, only comfier. I wear it under slip dresses, swimsuits, and on days where I don’t want to be bothered with bras. But, choosing the right boob tape is a very personal decision, if you know what I mean. So, to help you out, I’m sharing the five best picks, according to Amazon shoppers — because there’s nothing like hearing from real women who’ve put them to test IRL.

    5 Best Under-$18 Boob Tapes on Amazon

    VBT Boop and Body Tape

    The VBT boob tape is Amazon’s best-selling option and has racked up more than 3,000 five-star ratings. It’s available in a beige shade, as well as various sizes. Not to mention it comes with reusable silicone nipple covers as well as some fashion adhesive. “I was very skeptical at first about the boob tape,” wrote one shopper. “I was pleasantly surprised that the boob tape actually worked. It held up my heavy boobs!!” They also mentioned the boob tape stayed “through an entire night of dancing” without looking like a “uni-boob.” Better yet, it’s 60 percent off during Amazon’s Presidents Day sale.

    Dacotton Breast-Lifting Boob Tape

    Ditch your push-up bra and reach for this lifting boob tape from Dacotton instead. The waterproof adhesive is made for bra sizes A through G and includes a bonus set of silicone nipple covers. The neutral color is nearly invisible under clothing while the stretchy, breathable material feels like a second skin. One DD-cup shopper wore this tape for “15 hours straight,” and, according to them, it didn’t budge at all. “I had the support I needed without bra straps,” they concluded. Another pleased reviewer removed the tape with zero pain, and that alone is a major win.  

    Dgesic Boob Tape

    Dgesic’s boob tape is a steal at just $7. Made of 95 percent cotton, the tape is light and airy, while the matte texture blends into the skin. Even more exciting, it’s water- and sweat-resistant, holding up in the stickiest situations. “I’ve tried other tapes, and this is the only one that has stayed on even after sweating [during] a hot Miami night,” an Amazon shopper raved. “It [makes] my boobs look amazing,” they concluded. A smaller-chested reviewer sung nothing but praise for the boob tape as well, claiming it gave her bust a “bit of a lift.” 

    No matter which boob tape you choose, it can be worn in a multitude of ways: Wrap it around your chest, turn it into a criss-cross shape, create individual straps, and so much more. Pro tip: Cut the tape into specific shapes to meet individual needs, and make it a multi-use product by using it as sports tape to protect your muscles or stave off blisters..
