    Following hot on the heels of both Wynn Las Vegas and Marquee’s residency announcements is Hakkasan Las Vegas, one of the city’s premiere party destinations. Situated at the MGM Grand, the club has been a staple of Las Vegas nightlife for years now and as always, they continue to impress with their roster of top tier talent.

    Made up of mostly returning DJs, the residency roster for 2016 boasts some of the biggest names in EDM, such as Tiësto, Armin van Buuren, The Chainsmokers, Above & Beyond, Martin Garrix, Steve Aoki and many more. Again, it’s mostly the same lineup that we saw in 2015, but given the talent that’s involved here, we’re certainly not complaining.
