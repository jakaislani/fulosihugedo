    Cole Kmet is a well-known football player in the United States. In professional American football, he is pursuing a career as a tight end.

    Cole Kmet, meanwhile, is a member of the Chicago Bears of the National Football League. As a senior football player in 2017, he competed in the US Army All-American Bowl.

    Cole Kmet was selected in the second round of the NFL Draft’s 43 overall picks in 2020. As we all know, CBS Sports rated him the top tight end in the draft, while Pro Football Focus rated Cole Kmet second.

    Cole Kmet College

    Cole Kmet is a well-educated footballer who attended St. Viator High School before enrolling at Notre Dame College.

    Cole Kmet Contract

    Cole Kmet revealed on January 9, 2020 that he would forego his senior college season and join the 2020 NFL Draft after a good amateur career at Notre Dame. The Chicago Bears selected him as the 43rd overall pick in the second round of the 2020 draft.

    Cole Kmet Salary

    Cole Kmet eventually signed a four-year rookie contract with the team on July 21, 2020. Cole’s base salary for the 2021 season is $954,445 according to his contract.

    Cole Kmet Family

    Moving on to his family, Cole Kmet is the eldest among the four children of his parents, Frank Kmet and Kandace Kmet. Cole has two younger brothers named Casey Kmet and Cooper Kmet, and a younger sister named Frankie Kmet.
