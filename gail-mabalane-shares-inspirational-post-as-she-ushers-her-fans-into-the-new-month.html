    “And suddenly, you know … it’s time to start something new, and trust the magic of beginnings. 😊🙏🏾

    #NewShades
    #NewWeek
    #NewMonth
    #ZoeCam 📸
    @hello_bril 🕶😍”

    Days ago, she celebrated her eight marriage anniversary. Taking to her social media handle on Instagram, she shared a photo from her marriage ceremony as she labelled her husband as her bestest friend.
